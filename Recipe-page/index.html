Like Mac and Cheese?

Want something that is ooey gooey and cheesy? This recipe is perfect for you if you like Mac and Cheese, it will take you on a whole new level of cheesy. It's simple and easy to make!

Ingredients you will need!

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 ½ cup water
  • 8 ounces pasta of choice
  • 2 ounces grated Romano Cheese
  • 4 ounces shredded American Cheese
  • 4 ounces shredded sharp chedder
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Paprika

Ready to cook? Here are the steps!

  1. Combine 1 cup milk and 1 ½ cups water in a sauce pan and bring to a simmer. Once the milk and water are simmering, add 8 ounces of pasta of your choice.
  2. On medium low heat, gently cook pasta until all liquid is absorbed.
  3. When the liquid is absorbed, put the cooked pasta on very low heat and add 4 ounces of shredded American cheese.
  4. Once the American cheese is melted, stir in additional 4 ounces of shredded sharp cheddar and 2 romano cheese. Add a little shake of cayenne and paprika, cover, and let sit for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir then put in a separate serving bowl.
